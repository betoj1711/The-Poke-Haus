About The Poke Haus

We’re a small, collector‑run shop in North San Antonio focused on the Pokémon Trading Card Game. We keep things simple: fair prices, clean presentation, fast payouts, and friendly communication. Most sales happen via our eBay store, and local meetups are welcome.

What We Sell

  • Curated lots & themed bundles
  • Raw & graded singles (NM or better)
  • Collector accessories (sleeves, stands, holders)
  • Trainer Skins™ — holographic debit/credit card skins
